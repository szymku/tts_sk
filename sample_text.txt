The Kokoro-82M model is a high-quality, open-source text-to-speech system that produces natural-sounding audio. With 82 million parameters, it delivers excellent results while remaining efficient and fast.

You can experiment with different voices, playback speeds, and output formats to find the perfect combination for your needs.

Some interesting features to try:

First, choose between male and female voices. Each voice has its own unique characteristics and tone. For example, Sarah has a clear and articulate style, while Adam provides a deep and authoritative sound.

Second, adjust the playback speed. Slower speeds are great for learning and careful listening, while faster speeds work well for consuming content quickly.

Third, select your preferred output format. WAV files offer the highest quality, while MP3 and OGG provide good quality with smaller file sizes.

Have fun experimenting with this text-to-speech tool, and feel free to try it with your own documents, articles, or creative writing!
